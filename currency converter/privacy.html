    Privacy Policy

    Last updated: 2025-03-01

    Smart‑rate (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle any information when you use our website.

    Information Collection

    We do not collect any personally identifiable information. We do not use cookies for tracking, and we do not store any data about you on our servers. All calculations happen locally in your browser.

    Third-Party Advertising

    We may use Google AdSense to display ads. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other sites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

    AdSense partners may collect non-personal information such as browser type, time stamp, and pages visited for ad reporting. For more details, see Google’s advertising policies.

    External APIs

    Our currency converter uses free public APIs (exchangerate.host, frankfurter.app, open.er-api.com). When you request a conversion, your browser contacts those services directly; we do not proxy the request. Those services may log your IP address according to their own privacy policies.

    Data Security

    We implement reasonable security measures, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

    Changes to This Policy

    We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page.

    Contact

    If you have questions, please contact us.
